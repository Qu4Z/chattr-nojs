SITE

A barebones chat client, focused on "minimum viable" design.

Simply go to the room you'd like in the url bar, like so: SITE/r/all, SITE/r/room, SITE/r/...

Quick links:
Default Room
Random Room

Just share the room URL with the people you'd like to chat with.
